# training-week-summary

## Purpose

Define the top-of-Training-tab "week summary" surfaces: the rolling 7-day Last 7 Days hero card, the 4-slot training Objectives row (auto-derived from the target race), and the multi-week Plan Adherence section. These sections give a glanceable summary of where the runner is right now (rolling) and how they're tracking against their auto-derived objectives over the last 4 calendar weeks.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Last 7 Days hero card on Training tab
The Training tab SHALL display a "Last 7 Days" hero card as its first section, providing a 2-second glanceable summary using a **rolling 7-day window** (today minus 6 days through today). The card SHALL include: (1) a compliance ring showing planned-vs-completed workout count within the 7-day window, (2) a volume progress bar showing km completed vs weekly target from the active phase, (3) a rolling 7-day-vs-prior-7-day delta generated by `generate_wow_sentence()` as a subtitle, and (4) the next planned workout from `_next_workouts()`. The hero card absorbs the standalone WoW card — there SHALL NOT be a separate week-over-week section. The rolling window ensures the dashboard is always active — no Monday reset to zero.

#### Scenario: Rolling window mid-week
- **WHEN** today is Wednesday, the last 7 days include 3 runs totaling 18km, and 2 planned workouts remain in the next 4 days
- **THEN** the hero card shows volume bar at 18km vs phase target, compliance ring at 3/5 (planned workouts in 7-day window), WoW subtitle comparing this 7d vs prior 7d, and the next planned workout

#### Scenario: Monday after a strong weekend
- **WHEN** today is Monday with a long run on Saturday and easy run on Sunday still in the 7-day window
- **THEN** the hero card shows those runs in the volume and compliance totals — not a reset to zero

#### Scenario: All planned workouts completed in window
- **WHEN** all planned workouts within the 7-day window are completed and volume exceeds target
- **THEN** the compliance ring shows full, volume bar is green and full, WoW subtitle shows rolling comparison

#### Scenario: No plan data
- **WHEN** no planned workouts exist in `planned_workouts` for the 7-day window
- **THEN** the compliance ring is omitted, volume bar still shows against phase target, next workout shows "No plan loaded — run `fit plan import` or sync Garmin Calendar"

#### Scenario: No activities in window
- **WHEN** no runs exist in the last 7 days
- **THEN** the hero card shows 0km volume bar and 0/N compliance, with the next planned workout if available

### Requirement: Training objectives row on Training tab
The Training tab SHALL display an objectives row with exactly **4 canonical slots**, always in this order: (1) Weekly Volume (km/wk), (2) Long Run (km), (3) Z2 Compliance (%), (4) Consistency (consecutive weeks with 3+ runs, ISO-week based). These 4 dimensions are the universal training pillars for any distance runner. Targets are **auto-derived only** — when a target race exists, `derive_objectives()` populates all 4 slots; when no target race exists, all 4 render as deactivated showing current values and a "Set a target race with `fit target set`" prompt. There is no manual goal path into these slots. Each active slot shows a progress bar with current value, target, gap-to-target, and traffic-light color (green >=90%, amber 70-89%, red <70%). Volume, Long Run, and Z2 current values are measured over the rolling 7-day window. Consistency (streak) uses ISO-week counting — it updates on Monday, not daily.

#### Scenario: Volume objective on track
- **WHEN** a target race exists, weekly volume target is 40km, and 38km completed in the rolling 7-day window (95%)
- **THEN** the volume card shows a green progress bar at 95%, gap text "2km to go"

#### Scenario: Z2 percentage below target
- **WHEN** Z2 target is 80% and actual Z2 time in the rolling 7-day window is 62%
- **THEN** the Z2 card shows a red progress bar at 62/80 (77.5%), gap text "18pp below target"

#### Scenario: No target race (all slots deactivated)
- **WHEN** no target race is set (and therefore no auto-derived objectives exist)
- **THEN** all 4 slots render as deactivated, each showing the current measured value and "Set a target race with `fit target set`"

#### Scenario: Target race set (all slots active)
- **WHEN** a target race exists and `derive_objectives()` has produced auto-derived targets
- **THEN** all 4 slots use the derived targets (e.g., "Peak volume 50-65km/wk" → Volume target = 50km) and are shown as active with progress bars

#### Scenario: Consistency streak with in-week progress
- **WHEN** the streak is 12 weeks and the runner has run 2 times so far this ISO week (threshold is 3)
- **THEN** the card shows "12 wks" as the streak value with a sub-label "2/3 runs this week — 1 more to keep streak" 

#### Scenario: Streak secured for the week
- **WHEN** the streak is 12 weeks and the runner has run 3 times this ISO week
- **THEN** the card shows "12 wks" with a sub-label "3 runs this week — streak secured, updates Monday"

#### Scenario: Streak at risk
- **WHEN** the streak is 12 weeks, it's Saturday, and the runner has run 1 time this ISO week
- **THEN** the card shows "12 wks" with a sub-label "1/3 runs this week — 2 more by Sunday" in amber

### Requirement: Multi-week plan adherence on Training tab
The Training tab SHALL display a plan adherence section covering the last 4 weeks. Each week SHALL show: week label (e.g., "W15"), planned vs actual workout count, planned vs actual volume, a compliance percentage from `compute_plan_adherence()`, and a one-line summary (e.g., "4/5 workouts, 32/35km, 1 unplanned easy run"). Weeks with <70% compliance SHALL be flagged amber; <50% flagged red.

#### Scenario: 4 weeks of adherence data
- **WHEN** 4 weeks of planned and actual workout data exist
- **THEN** 4 week rows render with compliance percentages, the current week labeled "Current week (in progress)"

#### Scenario: Perfect adherence week
- **WHEN** a week has 5/5 planned workouts completed, no unplanned runs, volume within 10% of target
- **THEN** that week shows 100% compliance in green

#### Scenario: Missed workouts
- **WHEN** a week has 2/5 planned workouts completed
- **THEN** that week shows 40% compliance in red with "3 workouts missed"

#### Scenario: No plan data
- **WHEN** no planned workouts exist for any of the last 4 weeks
- **THEN** the section shows "No plan data. Import a plan with `fit plan import` or sync Garmin Calendar."

#### Scenario: Fewer than 4 weeks of data
- **WHEN** only 2 weeks of plan data exist
- **THEN** only 2 week rows render, no empty placeholders
